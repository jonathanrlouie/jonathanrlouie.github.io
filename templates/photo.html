Photoblog

Over the last few years, I have become more and more drawn in by the genre of street photography. I (unfortunately) do not have enough time to practice this as much as I want, but I do try to carry a camera around at all times.

I tend to work around what is "real" and candid, in other words, what is really there, on the streets. Within that framework, however, I am fascinated by things that seem unreal, dreamlike and/or eerie.

This is pretty much a mirror of my instagram, flickr and tumblr accounts.
